Description

Air Vanguard is the technical aviation series that appeals to warplane enthusiasts across the world. Each title gives a concise history of an aircraft, detailing its design, development and deployment.

This range of books was created by the the feedback of Osprey-Fans and aircraft-enthusiasts, who would like to see more technical details of single military aircraft. Every single Air Vanguard title delivers a precise overview on a special type of aircraft, design and development of the plane as well as modifications and improvements on later variants. The reader gets also a combat history of a special type of military airplane. The single titles contain a novel hinged back cover to exhibit th extraordinary Osprey plan drawings and the proven compilation of photographs and artistic colour pictures including combat scenes.
