Description

Beautiful illustrations, line drawings, b/w photos, tables, maps, organizational line charts and 8-full page color WW2 era color photos. These books are long Out-of-Print and quite scarce. These books are particularly sought after by collectors since the color illustrations are situational in nature compared to the generally static, analytical illustrations in the New Vanguard series which currently are in print
