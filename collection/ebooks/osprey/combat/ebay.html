Description

From the treacherous jungles of Vietnam to the hedgerows of Normandy, each title in the bestselling Combat series pits history’s greatest adversaries against one another, revealing what it was actually like for opposing warriors to face off against each other on the field of battle.

Each book contains:
• In-depth descriptions of three key engagements
• Innovative split-screen artwork
• Photographs and illustrations
• Tactical and strategic maps
• Battlescenes and figure plates

Combat pits history's adversaries against one another, from the snows of World War II's Eastern Front to the mud of the Somme. Combat's innovative split-screen artwork showing a key moment in battle from both sides' perspective offers a glimpse of war through the eyes of those who fought, while first hand accounts, expert analysis, photographs, maps and specially commissioned colour plates combine to immerse you in the experience of close quarter battles.
